## The Context Problem

<CodeBlock title="naive-agent.py">
{`# The naive approach — dump everything
context = read_entire_codebase()
response = llm.complete(
    system=context,  # 2M tokens of noise
    prompt=user_task
)
# Result: slow, expensive, unfocused`}
</CodeBlock>

> Throwing more context at the problem doesn't solve it.
> **Relevance beats volume, every time.**

<Notes>This is what most teams start with. It's the brute force approach and it fails for three reasons: cost, latency, and signal-to-noise ratio.</Notes>
