Why governance-driven architecture adoption fails — and what actually works when you're responsible for architecture across teams who have a hundred other priorities.

Through fictional (but painfully familiar) Slack conversations, this article walks through three phases every architect goes through: **The Evangelist** who publishes guidelines nobody reads, **The Enforcer** who adds gates nobody respects, and **The Enabler** who finally cracks the code — 80% inspiration, 20% automation.
